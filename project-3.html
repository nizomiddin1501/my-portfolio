Employee Management System

This project is built using spring boot and documented using swagger ui. The project regulates the working time, salary, pension and vacation processes of employees.

Live Link

Project Overview

The project automates the collection of various information about employees. The system helps the accountant and human resource manager to perform various accounting and information operations.

Functional capabilities of the system:
1. Issue with the total work rate of the same pinflies who worked more than n work rate in the requested month(1).
2. Issue with the total organization number and total monthly salary of the same pinflies who worked in different regions in the requested month(1).
3. Output with the average monthly salary of all organizations, all employees belonging to the given organization in the requested month(1) and the emloyee and his organization information.
4. Data of employees who received salary (SALARY) and vacation (VACATION) in the requested month (1) and issuance of received salary and vacation money.
